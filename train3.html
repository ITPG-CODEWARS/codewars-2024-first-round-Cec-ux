Rajdhani Express

The Rajdhani Express is one of India's fastest trains, linking New Delhi with key cities, offering premium service and speeds up to 140 km/h.

Features:

Fast Connectivity

Priority Routing

Onboard Catering

Air-Conditioned Comfort

Overview:

The Rajdhani Express is one of the fastest and most prestigious trains in India, connecting the nation's capital, New Delhi, with other important cities. Launched in 1969, it was the first high-speed train of India. Rajdhani trains prioritize comfort and speed, with air-conditioned coaches, catering, and premium service. It’s widely considered a premium option in India’s extensive rail network.
