Overview:

The Shinkansen, often referred to as the "bullet train," is famous for its speed, punctuality, and comfort. Japan’s Shinkansen network was the world’s first high-speed railway, inaugurated in 1964. It’s known for being ultra-reliable, rarely deviating by more than a few seconds from its schedule. The sleek design and advanced technology have made it a model for other high-speed rail systems around the world.
